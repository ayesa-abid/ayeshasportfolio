001
Lawyer
Connect
FlaskPython Real-TimeAuth
Problem

Legal help is inaccessible. Finding the right lawyer is a broken process — slow, opaque, and untrustworthy for people who need it most.

Thinking

A matching system lives or dies by trust infrastructure. Designed around secure auth, structured profiles, and low-latency communications as core pillars.

System

Legal matching platform with real-time messaging, role-based access control, and end-to-end secure authentication built in Flask.

Impact

A product that removes friction from access to justice. The architecture scales to any professional matching domain — legal, medical, financial.

002
University
Marketplace
Node.jsPostgreSQL P2PMessaging
Problem

University economies are informal and inefficient. Students buy and sell with no system to support trust, discovery, or accountability.

Thinking

Peer-to-peer platforms live or die by trust signals. Designed around reputation, real-time messaging, and frictionless listing flows from the ground up.

System

Scalable backend with user profiles, in-app messaging, and product listings built for concurrency — handles high request volume without degradation.

Impact

A community economy platform that made campus commerce legible and trustworthy. A replicable model for any closed-network marketplace.

003
NetGraph
Optimizer
C++BFS/DFS AVL TreeRouting
Problem

Network routing is computationally expensive. Most solutions don't balance speed and path quality simultaneously under real-world constraints.

Thinking

Graph traversal at the core — BFS for breadth, DFS for depth, AVL trees for self-balancing search at every decision node.

System

A graph-based routing engine combining classical algorithms with balanced tree structures for high-speed, high-accuracy path decisions in C++.

Impact

Proved that classical CS theory translates directly into real-world optimization. Algorithmic thinking isn't academic — it's engineering with taste.
